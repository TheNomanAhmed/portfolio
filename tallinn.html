A Day Trip to Tallinn

  • by:Noman Ahmed
  • date:August 6th, 2018
  • 0 comments

1st of April 05:00 in the morning Abu Zar, Bashir, Shahid one and two decided to take me on the trip to Tallinn; whereas i haven’t bought the ticket yet. I first laid my eyes on Tallinn November 2016; At the time, I was on my first solo trip to Helsinki, the Finnish capital situated 80 kilometers north of Tallinn. Given their close proximity and shared heritage, these two cities are often referred to as twin capitals, Baltic sisters, or simply Talsinki. This was my second time to Tallinn and first trip with my very close friends and i didn’t wanted to miss a single moment of it. That was the moment when i went straight into the Bus to take the 8:30 ferry with my friends to Tallinn. I was also lucky that i got a ticket by chance at the harbor and the fun started right after the ferry depart.

After 2 hours of sailing in the gulf of Finland, we finally reached to the beautiful Capital of Estonia. Where the first thing we did was grabbed the map and start to bounce between the famous monuments, churches and museums.
